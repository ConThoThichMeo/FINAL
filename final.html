Whisper to you


Savor delectable cuisine while reveling in the mesmerizing panorama of Tay Ho Lake at The Redamancy Restaurant – an enchanting culinary destination in Hanoi



A Dreamy Dining Destination

Redamancy offers an opulent and intimate ambiance, affording an idyllic backdrop for romantic rendezvous, familial gatherings, or special commemorative occasions. The meticulously designed environment imparts a sense of comfort and repose to all discerning patrons.

Perfect Choice For Special Occasions

We take immense pride in presenting a diverse gastronomic repertoire, spanning from venerable culinary traditions to avant-garde, palate-pleasing innovations, ensuring to gratify even the most refined tastes. Each culinary creation at Redamancy is curated with an ardor and reverence for premium ingredients, bestowing upon you an unparalleled epicurean experience.

A Tranquil Dating Experience

Beyond mere repasts, Redamancy aspires to orchestrate a symphony of romance, where love harmoniously intertwines with the art of gastronomy, yielding moments of euphoric rapture. We firmly believe that gastronomy serves as an exquisite medium to articulate love, and it is our fervent wish to bequeath upon you endearing and indelible moments.
